Selected: Dashboard


The Dashboard is your Summary and it shows you the current status of your inventory. This is where you get all your stock notifictions. Notifications are divided into 2 groups.

  1. 1. Stock Level Notifications
  2. 2. Expiry Notifiation

Stock Level Notifications

The Heading is self explanatory. This is where you will get notified of any stock that is:

  1. 1. Out of Stock
  2. 2. Below Min Stock
  3. 3. Over Max Stock
  4. 4. Approaching Min Stock
  5. 5. At Minimun Stock

NB: You will also find links below the Stock Level Notifications that lead you a specific list of the selected only notififcation

Expiry Notifications

The Heading is self explanatory. This is where you will get all Expiry Related Notifications listed below:

  1. 1. Expired Stock
  2. 2. Expiring Today
  3. 3. Expiring this Week
  4. 4. Expiring this Month

NB: You will also find links below the Expired Notifications that lead you a specific list of the selected only notififcation
